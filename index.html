About Me

Hi, I’m someone who’s into pretty much every sport — especially cycling, running, and football. Just don’t ask me to play chess... my son has already claimed the household championship title, and I’ve accepted my place.

I’ve spent years working in enterprise networking, and I’ve always had a soft spot for technology and computers. Outside of work, I’ve somehow become the go-to IT guy for my family and neighbours — if it plugs in, boots up, or connects to Wi-Fi, it’s probably passed through my hands at some point.

When I’m not solving tech mysteries or fine-tuning someone’s laptop, you’ll probably find me watching a good movie or series — or going down a rabbit hole that starts with one whitepaper and ends way past midnight.

Skills and Expertise

  • Computer Networks maintenance and configuration (Wired, Wireless and all the buzzwords like routing, switching, VPNs, DNS, SD-WAN ...)
  • Once cycled over 100 km just to see if I could (spoiler: I could, but my legs disagreed the next day).
  • I manage the tech support hotline for my entire family — payment accepted in coffee and homemade cake.
  • I strongly believe in sustainability, open systems, freedom, and that orange coins might fix more than you think.
